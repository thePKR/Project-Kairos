# Governance: SKI and Project Kairos

## 1. The SKI Framework

**SKI** is the apex parent company and organizational body governing the strategic deployment, commercial partnerships, and sovereign architecture of the network.

*   **Role:** SKI acts as the shielding entity, maintaining the operational integrity and legal defense of the decentralized structures beneath it.
*   **Monetization:** SKI handles all enterprise licensing, managed edge deployments, and private "Sovereign AI" consultations without claiming ownership over the open network itself.

## 2. Project Kairos: The Incubator

**Project Kairos** is the "baby company" or primary operational division managed by SKI. It functions as the continuous R&D and manufacturing engine.

*   **Role:** Kairos is the Autonomous Software Factory. It is responsible for the design, training, and iterative release of the Small Language Models (SLMs).
*   **The Release Cycle:** Under the direction of SKI, Kairos constantly churns out improved versions (e.g., Kairos SLM v1, v2, v3). Because the training loop runs at $0 cost via decentralized or free-tier infrastructure, the factory never stops evolving.
*   **The Open Commons:** The outputs of Project Kairos (such as the Rust inference engine and baseline model architectures) are released under the **AGPL-3.0 License**, ensuring they remain an open standard that "no one is able to claim."

## 3. The Unclaimable Strategy

By separating the **Entity (SKI)** from the **Engine (Kairos)**, we achieve an unclaimable business design:

1.  **If the code is copied:** The AGPL-3.0 license demands that any commercial SaaS use of Kairos' engine forces the copier to open-source their entire stack. SKI remains unaffected, as it profits from deployment and architecture, not mere software hoarding.
2.  **If a claim is attempted:** Project Kairos is an autonomous, iterational process. You cannot "patent" or "claim" the rapid, endless deployment of $0 SLMs. By the time a corporate entity attempts to lock down v2, Kairos has already been directed by SKI to release v3.

## 4. Director's Mandate

As the Director, your focus resides at the SKI level—orchestrating partnerships, defining high-level constraints, and guiding the continuous delivery objectives of the Kairos engine below.
